The Strengths and Weaknesses of Under Armour

Pro’s: The availability of Under Armour is currently rising, apparently you can find this product in over 25000 retailers, especially in large sport retail chains such as Dick’s Sporting Goods and Footlocker. Having popular representatives for their product Under Armour is gradually increasing their popularity, by having Stephen Curry, Cam Newton, Tom Brady and Michael Phelps are endorsing their brand they are increasing their popularity world wide. Under Armour has high comfortability for their products in the shoes and also their clothing and accessories. Their product does not cost as much as their leading competition in popularity, their shoes have a cost of around $50-150. Under Armour has amazing discounts for their products in Dick’s Sporting Goods which many of the sales can go up to around 75% off because of their clearance.

Con’s: Under Armour is not that attractive and it shows in the income of their product which is the reason for their shoe designs having no potential “eye catchers” to most people who want to buy their product. This unattractiveness does not allow their product to be as popular as their leading competition Nike, Jordan and Adidas. Under Armour doesn't have any collaborations with any designer brands or any other brands period which is another downfall if you ant to buy their product to wear as an everyday thing since their product is already so cheap. In conclusion Under Armour has many beneficial pros and cons that can be inferred.
